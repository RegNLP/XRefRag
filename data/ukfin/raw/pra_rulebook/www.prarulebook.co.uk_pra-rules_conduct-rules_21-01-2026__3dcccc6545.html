1

Application and Definitions

1.1

  1. (1) This Part applies to every function a person (P) performs in relation to a firm (A) that is:
    1. (a) a CRR firm;
    2. (b) a credit union; or
    3. (c) a third country CRR firm in relation to:
      1. (i) the activities of its establishment in the UK; or
      2. (ii) if it does not have an establishment in the UK, activities in the UK.
  2. (2) This Part only applies if P:
    1. (a) is approved under section 59 of FSMA to perform a senior management function in relation to A;
    2. (b) is an employee of A that should have been so approved;
    3. (c) is an employee or director who is performing a function that would have been a controlled function but for Senior Management Functions 2.3;
    4. (d) performs a certification function in relation to A;
    5. (e) is a Conduct Rules non-executive director of A; or
    6. (f) is a person in relation to whom a notice under section 59ZZA has been or could be given by the PRA to an authorised person.
  3. (3) 3.1 to 3.3 only apply to a person in (2)(a), (b), or (f).
  4. (4) 3.4 only applies to a person in (2)(a), (b), (e) or (f).
  • 31/12/2020
  • Past version of 1.1 before 31/12/2020
  • Legal Instruments that change this rule 1.1

1.2

In this Part, the following definitions shall apply:

director

means, in relation to a firm, a person who is a member of the board of directors of the firm or, if there is no such board, the equivalent body responsible for the management of the firm.

employee

has the meaning given in Certification 1.2.

senior management function

means either a PRA senior management function or an FCA designated senior management function.

Individual Conduct Rules

2.1

Individual Conduct Rule 1: You must act with integrity.

  • 07/03/2016
  • Legal Instruments that change this rule 2.1

Individual Conduct Rule 2: You must act with due skill, care and diligence.

3

Senior Manager Conduct Rules

3.1

Senior Manager Conduct Rule 1: You must take reasonable steps to ensure that the business of the firm for which you are responsible is controlled effectively.

  • 07/03/2016
  • Legal Instruments that change this rule 3.1

3.2

Senior Manager Conduct Rule 2: You must take reasonable steps to ensure that the business of the firm for which you are responsible complies with the relevant requirements and standards of the regulatory system.

  • 07/03/2016
  • Legal Instruments that change this rule 3.2

3.3

Senior Manager Conduct Rule 3: You must take reasonable steps to ensure that any delegation of your responsibilities is to an appropriate person and that you oversee the discharge of the delegated responsibility effectively.

  • 07/03/2016
  • Legal Instruments that change this rule 3.3

3.4

Senior Manager Conduct Rule 4: You must disclose appropriately any information of which the FCA or PRA would reasonably expect notice.

  • 07/03/2016
  • Legal Instruments that change this rule 3.4
